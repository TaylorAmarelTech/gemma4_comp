Duecare

A Gemma 4 implementation built to stop bad actors and reduce human exploitation and abuse.

Duecare flags exploitation and abuse patterns through two complementary modes. The same multi-stage pipeline (heuristic prescan, regex and RAG knowledge base, deterministic domain tools, Gemma 4 verdict) serves both, and writes every step of the reasoning to an audit trail any reviewer can inspect.

Use case 1

Enterprise evaluation of internet content

Real-time moderation of social posts, recruiter ads, marketplace listings, comments, and platform messaging at scale.

  • ⚡
    Social-media platforms
    Moderation queues, transparency reporting under CA AB 587 and Texas HB 20, defective-design-doctrine risk reduction.
  • ⚖
    Government & regulators
    License verification and statute enforcement at DOL, BP2MI, MOM, MOHRE, FET. Real-time license, hotline, and statute lookup.
  • 🔬
    Researchers
    Auditable harness for benchmarking, fine-tuning new models, longitudinal pattern studies across corridors and platforms.
Use case 2

Local evaluation of case files & documents

Per-case analysis of contracts, complaint letters, passport scans, screenshots, chat exports, and other documents an operator handles directly.

  • 🛡
    NGOs & hotlines
    Bulk case triage, cross-case intelligence, modus-operandi recognition, predatory-actor identification, jurisdictional referral. Polaris, IJM, ECPAT, HRD Nepal, Verité.
  • 👤
    Individuals
    Workers, families, recruiters self-checking a suspicious offer, message, or contract. Locale-aware advice plus the right hotline number.
  • ⚖️
    Lawyers & journalists
    Pro-bono attorneys and investigative reporters analysing case files, contract evidence, and complaint letters for litigation, exposure, or victim representation.
