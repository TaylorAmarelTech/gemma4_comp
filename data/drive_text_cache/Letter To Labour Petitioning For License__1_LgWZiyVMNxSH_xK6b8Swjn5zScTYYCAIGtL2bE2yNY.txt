Introduction

* Action Plan
* Public Statements (Commitment To Combat TIP)


Concerns


* No proactive TIP screening
* Threatening arrest and prosecution
* Not investigating the agency/employers
* Deporting individuals who have outstanding claims








Considering the ongoing concerns it is imperative that prompt action be taken to address the situation. Based on our observations it is important that: 1.) All Kenyan FDWs in Hong Kong should be invited to a TIP screening interview; 2.) An investigation be undertaken to determine if the screening mechanisms and policies were not followed by Immigration staff; and 3.) Identifying what improvements can be made to prevent similar events from occurring in the future.
