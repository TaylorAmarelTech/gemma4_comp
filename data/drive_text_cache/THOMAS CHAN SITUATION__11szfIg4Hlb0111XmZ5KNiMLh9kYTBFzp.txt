Dear Sirs,
After reading the latest Annual Return filed by HONG KONG UNION OF EMPLOYMENT
AGENCIES LIMITED (HKUEA), it is believed that the company, its directors, and the corporate
secretary may be involved in a scheme to submit false information to the Companies Registry
(CR).
According to the CR, “Making a false statement in any document delivered for registration” is an
example of a breach of the Companies Ordinance (CO). Given such, it is alleged that violations
of the CO have occurred because known-to-be-false and inaccurate information was submitted
as the “Residential Address” for all directors of HKUEA.
Based on information and belief, none of the directors of HKUEA are related to each other, nor
do they live at the same address. However, all 10 directors of HKUEA list “G/F., Blk B, Sunny
Terace, 102 Fu Tei Pai, Fanling N.T.” as their residential address. This is very suspicious - it
is almost impossible that 10 unique individuals would be living at a single address as
implied by reading the information within the latest annual return for HKUEA.
It is requested that the CR open an investigation into this information and confirm whether these
are valid residential address or not. Furthermore, it is also noted that CHAN Tung Fung is also a
director of ASOSIASI PERUSAHAAN PENEMPATAN TENAGA KERJA TERAMPIL
INDONESIA HONG KONG & MACAO LIMITED (Company Number: 2329878) which has 5 of 7
directors sharing the same “Residential Address”.
Thank you for your time and attention to this matter.
Why are so many directors listed at one address? This looks like a Companies Ordinance
violation.
