	Report Of Suspected Money Laundering & Request For Assistance


To Whom This May Concern,


We are writing to report a suspected money laundering scheme[1]and request assistance in obtaining refunds for victims of the illegal scheme.


Based on the enclosed information, it is requested that:

1. All parties involved immediately cease and desist from processing financial transactions involving tainted money;
2. All parties involved take prompt action to facilitate refunds to all involved victims.


By way of background a number of individuals (collectively referred to as “suspects”) are operating an unlicensed money lending business which grants excessive interest loans to individuals in Hong Kong. 


________________
The suspects:


1. Grant loans without a money lenders license in contravention to the Money Lenders Ordinance;
2. Grant loans that carry excessive interest in contravention to the Money Lenders Ordinance;
3. Engage in suspect fraud, blackmail, and other behavior likely constituting criminal intimidation/harassment;
4. Actively violate provisions of the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance; and
5. Refuse to provide their physical address or any background information about their lending operations.
6. Share phone numbers, QR codes, and bank accounts with RoboCash Limited, a Hong Kong company with the following website address: https://robocash.group/ 


Excessive Interest (Predicate Indictable Offense):


Pursuant to the Money Lenders Ordinance, granting a loan with an interest rate exceeding 60% per annum constitutes an indictable offense. The suspects regularly grant loans with interest rates far exceeding the maximum rate of 60% per annum. The loans are usually structured in the following way:


Loan Amount
	2,000 HKD
	Processing Fee
	200 HKD
	Loan Principal[2]
	1,800 HKD
	Total Repayment
	6,000 HKD
	Repayment Schedule
	300 HKD per week for 20 weeks
	Length of Loan
	20 weeks or 0.383561644 of a year
	Interest Charges
	4,200 HKD
	Annual Interest Rate
	606.6684% per year
	

606.6684% per annum, is over 10 times the maximum allowable interest of 60% per year. Given the significant number of accounts, transfer mechanisms, and suspicious indicators present[3] - there is no excuse as to why banks, MSOs, and SVFs continue to process transactions for this tainted and highly illicit unlicensed and excessive interest lending scheme.

Prompt action must be taken to remedy this situation and process refunds for myself and the other victims.
