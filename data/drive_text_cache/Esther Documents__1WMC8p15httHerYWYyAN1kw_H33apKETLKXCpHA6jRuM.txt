1. Take elevator to the appropriate floor

2. Wait in waiting area

3. When registration room is open go to the registration room

4. Wait for your name/case to be called and raise your hand

5. Wait for your name to be called again and go to the registration desk

6. The registration desk will ask for the latest Annual Return of the Defendant, please provide this document to the registration desk

7. The registration desk will also ask if you want to engage in a settlement discussion with the other party, you can answer yes or no

8. Afterward you will be invited into a separate room with a court office that will ask you to summarize your case. Be prepared to answer questions about the:

   1. Dates certain things happened
   2. The cause of action
   3. The calculation of damages
   4. The pages that critical evidence is on

      9. After you finish speaking with the court officer you will be directed to wait in the courtroom.

      10. Wait until the judge arrives and calls your name/case

      11. Proceed to the Claimant table and sit down

      12. Judge will ask you “What is your cause of action”

      13. Judge may ask you the following other questions:
         1. Do you need to submit more evidence
         2. How long do you need to reply to the Defendant’s defense
         3. Will you be available to testify
         4. How are you calculating damages
         5. What address was used to serve the Defendant
         6. When did you send documents to the court
