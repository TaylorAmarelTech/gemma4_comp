1. Complainants request an update on the status of their case against God’s Will International Placement Inc for which no substantive action has been taken since the conciliation-mediation proceedings of 29 January 2021, during which the parties failed to reach an agreement and the case was forwarded by the Department of Labour and Employment (“DOLE”) to the Philippines Overseas Employment Administration (“POEA”). 2. Complainants also affirm that it is Complainants desire and intention to provide evidence and testimony to this forum regardless of the result of any civil settlement, indeed it is in the interest of the public and justice to ensure law enforcement and administrative bodies receive all evidence in the performance of their regulatory functions. Given the serious nature of the Respondent’s inappropriate activities, including but not limited to, referrals to exclusive partners, conspiracy with an illicit money lender, POEA rule violations, and internationally recognized money laundering and human trafficking activities, Complaiants request this Forum take immediate appropriate action.
