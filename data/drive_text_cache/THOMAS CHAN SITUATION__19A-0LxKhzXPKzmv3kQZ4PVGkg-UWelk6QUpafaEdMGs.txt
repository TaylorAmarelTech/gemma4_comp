Dear Sirs,

After reading the latest Annual Return filed by ASOSIASI PERUSAHAAN PENEMPATAN TENAGA KERJA TERAMPIL INDONESIA HONG KONG & MACAO LIMITED (APPTKTI), it is believed that the company, its directors, and the corporate secretary may be involved in a scheme to submit false information to the Companies Registry (CR).

According to the CR, “Making a false statement in any document delivered for registration” is an example of a breach of the Companies Ordinance (CO). Given such, it is alleged that violations of the CO have occurred because known-to-be-false and inaccurate information was submitted as the “Residential Address” for at least 5 directors of APPTKTI.


Based on information and belief, none of the directors of HKUEA are related to each other, nor do they live at the same address. However, 5 directors of APPTKTI list “Flat E, 1/F., Hing Fat Building, 11 Yu King Square, Yuen Long, N.T.” as their residential address. This is very suspicious - it is almost impossible that 5 non-related business partners would be living at a single address as implied by reading the information within the latest annual return for APPTKTI.


It is requested that the CR open an investigation into this information and confirm whether these are valid residential addresses or not. Furthermore, it is also noted that CHAN Tung Fung is also a director of HONG KONG UNION OF EMPLOYMENT AGENCIES LIMITED (Company Number: 1999103) which had 10 directors sharing the same “Residential Address” and which was the subject of a similar complaint.

Thank you for your time and attention to this matter.
