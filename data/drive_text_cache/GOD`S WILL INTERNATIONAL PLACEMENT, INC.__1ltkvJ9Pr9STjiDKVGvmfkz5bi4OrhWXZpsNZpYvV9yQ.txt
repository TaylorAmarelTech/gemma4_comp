 Notes on payment required by agency


# 1. Training: 


Respondent imposed an exclusive and compulsory arrangement whereby the Complainant was required to pay for and attend a designated training session at Right Focus Training Center and Starlight Review Center.  Respondent required Complainant to pay 18,000  PHP for Training at Right Focus Training Center and 16,500 PHP for Enrichment Training at Starlight Review Center, amounting a total of 34,500 PHP. Complainant should be given a full refund of the 34,500 PHP fee. According to the 2016 POEA Rules and Regulations training, if required, must be paid by the employer or agency and the only legitimate fee OFWs can be charged for in relation to training is the fee for a “Certificate of Competency” and paying for training courses is not a requirement to obtain a “Certificate of Competency” especially for a common skill such as domestic work. 


# 2. Medical: 
Respondent imposed an exclusive and compulsory arrangement whereby Complainant was required to attend medical examinations at Right Med Diagnostic Center and Ilo-Ilo PDS Laboratory Center. Complainant was given no opportunity to choose any other medical clinic, and was required to go to the medical center referred to by the agency. As she was not given any other option other than this medical clinic, the Complainant suspects that the agency is benefitting by overcharging and taking a commission for the referral. In total the Complainant made payments totalling  PHP. 8,300 PHP


Given the exclusive referral of Respondent, Complainant had no choice but to make payments for the medical examinations and was not given a choice to take the exam at other medical clinics. If it was not for the coercion and threats from Respondent Complainant and other prospective OFWs would have never chosen to pay 8,300 PHP because:

1. 8,300  PHP far exceeds the 2,000 - 2,200 PHP medical check fee limit set by the Department of Health in Annex K of A.O. No. 2013-0006, “Basic DOH PEME for Overseas Work Applicants”; 
2. Pursuant to Clause 8 of Complainant’s Standard Employment Contract, the medical check fees must be paid by the employer. 


Therefore, the Complainant should be given a full refund of 8,300 PHP medical fee.
