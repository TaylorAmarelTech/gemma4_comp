I am writing this report to law enforcement authorities and officers of Pacific Ace Finance Limited and affiliates (collectively “Pacific Ace”) to report suspected violations of:


1. Section 11 and Section 12 of the Banking Ordinance;
2. Section 19, Section 22, and other provisions of the Money Lenders Ordinance;
3. Condition 6, 10, and 12 of the Licensing Conditions for Money Lenders.


I have previously reported these concerns to the Compliance Officer of Pacific Ace and law enforcement authorities yet no apparent action has been taken, as a result the suspected unlawful and illicit activities of Pacific Ace continue with observable malice and disregard for the law.


Section 11 and Section 12 of the Banking Ordinance


Pacific Ace has been offering and advertising deposit taking accounts despite the fact that Pacific Ace does not have a license from the Hong Kong Monetary Authority and is thus not authorized to engage in the banking business.

Section 19, Section 22, and other provisions of the Money Lenders Ordinance

1. Refused to provide a copy of the loan agreement despite repeated written requests. After a one month default on a request for a loan agreement Pacific Ace continues to engage in prohibited collection activities in violation of Section 19 of the Money Lenders Ordinance;
2. Attempted to enforce an agreement where the interest rate increases upon default in violation of Section 22 of the Money Lenders Ordinance.
Condition 6, 10, and 12 of the Licensing Conditions for Money Lenders


For multiple months Pacific Ace has engaged in a scheme to harass me, my co-borrower, my employer, and my co-borrower’s employers by among other things, making repeated and harassing calls to third parties without permission, claiming individuals are references without written authorization from those references, and threatening others, such as my co-borrower, in a malicious effort to extract personal data from others without authorization. Among other things, Pacific Ace has repeatedly called my employer without permission, Pacific Ace stores, processes, and maintains my employer’s personal data along with the personal data of other third parties despite these third parties (i) not being a party to the loan agreement; and (ii) never providing Pacific Ace with written consent. Such activities constitute serious violations of the Licensing Conditions for Money Lenders as well as the Personal Data Privacy Ordinance and potentially the Crime ordinance as well.
